Why Your Support Matters

Building a union takes more than just workers—it takes a community.

✊

Strength in Numbers

Every signature, every voice, every act of solidarity adds to our collective power. When the community stands with workers, employers take notice.

🌉

Building Bridges

Labor struggles connect to broader movements for economic justice, education equity, and community wellbeing. Your support helps build those connections.

📣

Amplifying Voices

When community members speak up for student workers, it sends a powerful message that our cause has broad support beyond the campus.

Confidentiality & Privacy

Your information is safe with us.


We take your privacy seriously. Information submitted through these forms is used only for union organizing purposes and is never shared with CCSF administration, supervisors, or any third parties.


Under labor law, you have the right to organize without interference from your employer. If you ever experience retaliation for union activity, contact us immediately—we're here to support you.


Questions? Reach out to us at ccsfswu@proton.me
